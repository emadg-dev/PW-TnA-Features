
اضافه‌کار (Overtime)
Work performed outside of the mandatory shift hours.

اضافه‌کار با مجوز (OvertimeWithPermit)
The portion of overtime that has been approved as permissible based on registered permits (either total or specific).

اضافه‌کار بدون مجوز (OvertimeWithoutPermit)
The portion of overtime that exceeds the allowed limit and requires manager approval.

نوع اضافه‌کار (OvertimeType)
An Enum specifying the overtime's position: Before (before the shift), Between (between work intervals), and After (after the shift).

مجوز کلی اضافه‌کار (overtimeTotal)
A general (Total) permit used to convert unauthorized overtime to authorized across all segments (before, between, after).

مجوز موردی اضافه‌کار (overtimeBefore, overtimeAfter)
Specific permits issued only for a particular part of the overtime (e.g., before the shift).

سقف اضافه‌کار پرسنل (MaxAllowedOvertimePerDay)
The maximum daily allowed overtime defined in the employee's profile.

سقف اضافه‌کار شیفت (MaxOverTimePerDay)
The maximum daily allowed overtime specified in the shift definition.

اضافه‌کار در روز تعطیل (MaxOvertimeOnHoliday)
The overtime limit for holidays.

اضافه‌کار بدون تایید (OvertimeWithoutApproval...)
The amount of overtime that does not require explicit permission and is automatically approved (for before, after, between, and holidays).
