========================================
SHIFT DOMAIN DICTIONARY
========================================

Shift
  A scheduled work period defined by Code, Title,
  StartTime, EndTime, and optionally DurationDays
  and AbsenceDayEquivalent.

Shift Code
  A unique numeric or alphanumeric identifier
  assigned to a shift.

Shift Title
  A unique descriptive name assigned to a shift.

Shift Start Time
  The clock time at which the shift begins.

Shift End Time
  The clock time at which the shift ends.
  Its validity may depend on DurationDays.

DurationDays
  The number of day boundaries between shift
  start and shift end.
  0 = shift must end after StartTime on the same day.
  1+ = shift may end on a later day.

Absence Day Equivalent
  A time value representing the equivalent
  duration counted as one absence day for the shift.

Shift Dependency
  A relationship where a shift is being used
  by another entity (e.g., Group Cycle),
  preventing certain operations like deletion.
