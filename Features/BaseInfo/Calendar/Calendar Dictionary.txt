========================================
CALENDAR MANAGEMENT DOMAIN DICTIONARY
========================================

Calendar Year
  A specific year (e.g., "1404") for which holiday calendar
  data can be generated and activated within the tenant.

Year Activation
  The process of marking a Calendar Year as active and generating
  its corresponding holiday calendar entries.

Active Year
  A Calendar Year that has generated holiday calendar entries.

Inactive Year
  A Calendar Year that has no generated holiday calendar entries.

Holiday Calendar Entry
  A daily calendar record for a specific date within a year,
  containing attributes such as isHoliday and isRamadan.

isHoliday
  A flag indicating whether a specific calendar date is considered
  an official, normal or special holiday.

isRamadan
  A flag indicating whether a specific calendar date falls within
  the Ramadan period.
