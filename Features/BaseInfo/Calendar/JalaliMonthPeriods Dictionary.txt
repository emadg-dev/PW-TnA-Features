========================================
CUSTOM JALALI MONTH PERIODS DOMAIN DICTIONARY
========================================

Jalali Month
  A calendar month in the Jalali (Persian) calendar system.

MonthStartDay
  A tenant-level setting that defines which day-of-month marks the start
  of each Jalali month period for payroll/attendance calculations.

Jalali Month Period
  A computed date interval used for payroll/attendance that represents
  a Jalali month according to MonthStartDay, not necessarily matching
  the natural month boundaries.
  
InvalidMonthStartDay
  A business error indicating MonthStartDay is outside the allowed range.
