

تاخیر ورود (LateArrivalTime)
The final lateness amount for an employee after applying all pardons, hourly leaves, and allowed tolerances.

تاخیر مجاز (AllowedDelay)
The amount of permissible delay specified in the shift definition (SingleDayShiftDto), applicable to all employees on that shift.

بخشودگی تاخیر (MaxDelayPerDay)
The personal forgivable lateness amount defined in the employee's profile (EmployeeDto).

**تعجیل (Early Departure)**

تعجیل خروج (EarlyLeaveTime)
The amount of time an employee leaves early before the end of their shift, after deducting any pardons.

تعجیل مجاز (AllowedEarlyDeparture)
The amount of permissible early departure specified in the shift definition (SingleDayShiftDto).

بخشودگی تعجیل (MaxHurryPerDay)
The personal forgivable early departure amount defined in the employee's profile (EmployeeDto).

**غیبت/عدم حضور (Absence)**

عدم حضور بین روز (AbsentSegmentDto)
Time intervals during work hours (between the first entry and last exit) where the employee was not present without a registered leave or mission.

مجموع غیبت (SumOfAbsentTime)
The total sum of an employee's absence time in one day.
